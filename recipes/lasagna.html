Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The meat sauce (ragù) is slow-cooked with tomatoes, garlic, and herbs, while the béchamel adds a velvety texture. Baked until bubbly and golden, it’s a crowd-pleaser for family dinners.

Traditionally, lasagna is baked at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes, covered with foil to prevent drying. Pro tip: Let it rest 15 minutes before slicing to hold its shape. The result is a hearty, cheesy masterpiece with perfectly balanced flavors!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  • Onion and garlic
  • Italian herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and meat; add tomato sauce and herbs to make ragù.
  2. Prepare béchamel sauce with butter, flour, and milk.
  3. Layer pasta, ragù, béchamel, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese on top.
  5. Bake covered at 375°F (190°C) for 30 mins, then uncovered for 15 mins.
  6. Rest 15 minutes before serving.
