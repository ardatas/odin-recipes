Beef Wallington

Description

Beef Wellington is a classic, elegant dish featuring a tender beef fillet wrapped in mushroom duxelles, prosciutto, and puff pastry. To prepare it, sear the fillet until browned, then coat it with a layer of finely chopped mushrooms, shallots, thyme, and a touch of mustard. Next, wrap it in thin slices of prosciutto to lock in moisture before encasing it in puff pastry and baking until golden.

raditionally, Beef Wellington is baked at around 400°F (200°C) for 20–25 minutes until the pastry is crisp and the beef is medium-rare. Serve it sliced, ideally with a rich red wine or truffle sauce. Pro tip: Chill the mushroom mixture before assembling to prevent soggy pastry. The result should be juicy, flavorful beef with a crispy, flaky crust—perfect for a special occasion!

Ingredients

  • Beef tenderloin
  • Pastry
  • Parma ham
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Mustard
  • Egg
  • Olive oil and Butter

Steps

  1. Sear seasoned beef tenderloin in oil until browned, then brush with Dijon mustard.
  2. Sauté mushrooms, shallots, garlic, and thyme into a dry duxelles mixture.
  3. Wrap the beef in prosciutto and mushroom duxelles, then chill to firm.
  4. Encase the bundle in puff pastry, seal edges, and brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 mins until golden and medium-rare.
  6. Rest 10 mins, slice, and serve with red wine sauce.
