Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

Garlic Butter Shrimp is a quick, flavorful seafood dish featuring succulent shrimp sautéed in a rich garlic, butter, and herb sauce. Ready in just 15 minutes, it's perfect over pasta, rice, or with crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce. The shrimp are cooked just until pink and tender, then tossed with lemon juice and parsley for a bright finish.

This dish works great as an appetizer or main course. Pro tip: Use fresh, large shrimp for the best texture, and don't overcook them! The result is restaurant-quality shrimp with an irresistible buttery garlic sauce that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Large shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • Butter
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Pat shrimp dry and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high.
  3. Sauté garlic for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add shrimp and cook 2-3 minutes per side until pink.
  5. Stir in lemon juice, parsley, and red pepper flakes.
  6. Serve immediately with sauce poured over top.
